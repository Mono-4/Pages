In this game, you can see the reflection of Laser.
Press space to switch between "laser mode" and "move mode."
The direction can be determined with four direction keys.

・current:Current number of reflection points
・max:Maximum number of reflection points
・max dot:You can change the maximum number of reflection points by entering characters
