This is a decryption game.
First, there is a sentence of a capital letter in uppercase. Change this sentence and change it to the correct answer.

▼Description of display

  • Upper text: Encryption
  • Red text in the middle: before num:before char[number of this word]
  • Bottom buttons: Used for changing characters

▼How to change characters.

  1. Enter the corresponding number of the character you want to change in "before num" .
  2. Enter the character after the change in "after small char".
  3. Then press "change".
Repeat this, and if "finish" is displayed, decoding is successful !
(Ultimately all characters must be small character.)
